Qst: Design and implement a program that converts a sum of money 
	    to a different currency. The amount of money to be converted, 
	    and the exchange rate, are entered by the user. The program 
	    should have separate methods for:
		    Obtaining the sum of money from the user;
		    Obtaining the exchange rate from the user;
		    Making the conversion;
		    Displaying the result.
