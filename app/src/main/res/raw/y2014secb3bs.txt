The keyword throws is used to specify a throws clause. The throws clause is placed after the closing parenthesis
of the method's parameters list. The throws keyword is followed by a comma-separated list of exception types that the method main will throw
if various problems occur. Such exceptions may be thrown by statements in the method's
body or by methods called from the body. Hence, the throws clause in the above code
indicate to the rest of the program that method main may throw an IOException
